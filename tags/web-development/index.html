Hugo Taxonomy Guide: Organizing Your Content

Hugo Taxonomy Guide: Organizing Your Content When building a website with Hugo, organizing your content effectively is crucial for both user experience and site management. Hugo’s taxonomy system provides a powerful way to categorize and tag your content, making it easier for users to find related information. What are Taxonomies in Hugo? Taxonomies in Hugo are user-defined groupings of content. By default, Hugo supports two taxonomies: Categories: Broad classifications of your content Tags: More specific descriptors for your content However, you can define custom taxonomies based on your specific needs, such as series, authors, or any other way you want to organize your content. ...
